### 📖 About Me

Vitor Santos is an Analyst and Developer specializing in multiplatform solutions and high-performance systems. With a decade of self-taught experience in backend development, he excels in building scalable architectures using **Spring Boot (Java/Kotlin), PHP (Laravel), and TypeScript (Node.js)**. 

He is dedicated to unifying server-side robustness with the agility of modern interfaces and embedded systems. With extensive experience in **AWS (Amazon Web Services)**, Vitor leverages cloud infrastructure to ensure high availability and performance. 

Currently based in **Salvador, Brazil**, his primary interests include **Software Architecture, Robotics, Information Security, Hardware Design, and IoT**. In his free time, he develops games with friends and teaches hardware/software to children in local schools.
